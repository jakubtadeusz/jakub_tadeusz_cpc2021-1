Hello!

On the left panel, you can choose between the three functions that can help you with your guitar progress!

1.) Chords creator

 Here you can create your own chords and save them in the chords subpage, or, if you want, you can also save the chord as an image.

2.) Guitar chords

 On this page, you can learn the patterns of the seven open chords (D, A, E, Am, Em, G, C) and view the chords you saved in the chords creator.
You can also select the chord transition patterns to open the metronome and train moving between different chords.

3.) Metronome

 Basic tool that plays the sounds in the selected intervals given the BPM (beats per minute) value.
You can set the BPM, add more beats to the bar or change between two sounds of the each beat.
